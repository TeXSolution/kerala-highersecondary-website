Question 6

Can a body have a charge \(8\times10^{-18}\) C

Answer

$$\begin{align} Q&=ne\\[6pt] n&=\frac{Q}{e}\\[6pt] n&=\frac{8\times10^{-18}}{1.6\times10^{-19}}\\[6pt] n&=50\\[6pt] n&=1,2,3,\ldots \end{align} $$

Question 7

Answer

\(+6\times10^{-9}\) C

Question 8

What is the radius of the circular path of an electron moving at a speed of \(3\times10^7\) m/s in a magnetic of \(6\times 10^{-4}\) T perpendicular to it?
\((e=1.6\times10^{-19}\,\mathrm{C} \text{ and } m=9.1\times10^{-31}\, \mathrm{kg})\)

Answer

$$\begin{align} V&=3\times10^7\,\mathrm{m}/\mathrm{s}\\[6pt] B&=6\times 10^{-4}\,\mathrm{T}\\[6pt] r&=\frac{Vm}{qB}\\[6pt] r&=\frac{3\times10^7\times9.1\times10^{-31}}{1.6\times10^{-19}\times6\times10^{-4}}\\[6pt] r&=\underline{\underline{0.28\,\mathrm{m}}} \end{align}$$
