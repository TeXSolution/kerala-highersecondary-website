Chapter 1 Sets

Question 1

Let \(\mathrm{U}=\{1,2,3,4,5,6\}\), \(\mathrm{A}=\{2,3\}\), \(\mathrm{B}=\{3,4,5\}\)

  1. Find \(\mathrm{A}',\mathrm{B}'\)
  2. Find \(\mathrm{A}\cup\mathrm{B},\mathrm{A}\cap\mathrm{B}\)
  3. Show that \((\mathrm{A}\cup\mathrm{B})'=\mathrm{A}'\cap\mathrm{B}'\)

Answer

  1. $$\begin{align} \mathrm{A}'&=\mathrm{U}-\mathrm{A}\\[6pt] \mathrm{A}'&=\underline{\underline{\{1,4,5,6\}}}\\[6pt] \mathrm{B}'&=\mathrm{U}-\mathrm{B}\\[6pt] \mathrm{B}'&=\underline{\underline{\{1,2,6\}}} \end{align}$$
  2. $$\begin{align} \mathrm{A}\cup\mathrm{B}&=\underline{\underline{\{2,3,4,5\}}}\\[6pt] \mathrm{A}\cap\mathrm{B}&=\underline{\underline{\{3\}}} \end{align}$$
  3. LHS
    $$\begin{align} (\mathrm{A}\cup\mathrm{B})'&=\mathrm{U}-(\mathrm{A}\cup \mathrm{B})\\[6pt] &=\{1,6\} \end{align}$$ RHS
    $$\begin{align} \mathrm{A}'\cap\mathrm{B}'&=\{1,6\}\\[6pt] LHS&=RHS \end{align}$$
    4. Hence verified.

    Question 2

    1. If \(\mathrm{A}=\{1,2,3\}\) and \(\mathrm{B}=\{1,4\}\), then the number of subsets A \(\times\) B is
      1. \(5^2\)
      2. \(6^2\)
      3. \(2^5\)
      4. \(2^6\)
    2. If \(\left(\frac{x}{3}+1,y-\frac{2}{3}\right)=\left(-\frac{2}{3},\frac{2}{3}\right)\), then find \(x\) and \(y\)

    Answer

    1. $$\begin{align} \text{number of subset}&=2^n\\[6pt] \text{number of subset of } \mathrm{A}\times\mathrm{B}&=2^{n(\mathrm{A})\times{n(\mathrm{B})}}\\[6pt] &=2^{3\times2}\\[6pt] &=\underline{\underline{2^{6}}} \end{align}$$
    2. $$\begin{align} \frac{x}{3}+1&=-\frac{2}{3}\\[6pt] \frac{x}{3}&=-\frac{2}{3}-1\\[6pt] \frac{x}{3}&=\frac{-2-3}{3}\\[6pt] \frac{x}{\cancel{3}}&=\frac{-5}{\cancel{3}}\\[6pt] x&=\underline{\underline{-5}}\\[6pt] y-\frac{2}{3}&=\frac{2}{3}\\[6pt] y&=\frac{2}{3}+\frac{2}{3}\\[6pt] y&=\underline{\underline{\frac{4}{3}}} \end{align}$$
