Chapter 7 Integrals

Question 1

$$\begin{equation}\int \sin x \sin (\cos x)dx\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &\text{Let} \quad\color{yellow}{\cos x=t}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{-\sin xdx=dt}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\sin xdx=-dt}\\[4pt] \int \sin x \sin (\cos x)dx&=\int\sin (t)\cdot -dt\\[6pt] &=-\int\sin (t)\cdot dt\\[6pt] &=-{-\cos t}+c\\[6pt] &=\cos t+c\\[6pt] &=\underline{\underline{\cos (\cos x)+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 2

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{1}{x+x\log x}dx\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &\text{Let }\quad\color{yellow}{1+\log x=t}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\frac{1}{x}dx=dt}\\[4pt] \int \frac{1}{x+x\log x}dx&=\int \frac{1}{x(1+\log x)}dx\\[6pt] &=\int\frac{1}{t}dt\\[6pt] &=\log \left\vert t\right\vert+c\\[6pt] &=\underline{\underline{\log \left\vert1+\log x\right\vert+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 3

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{{\sec}^2x}{\sqrt{\tan^2x+4}}dx\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &\text{Let }\quad\color{yellow}{\tan x=t}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\sec^2xdx=dt}\\[4pt] \int \frac{{\sec}^2x}{\sqrt{\tan^2x+4}}dx&=\int \frac{dt}{\sqrt{t^2+4}}dx\\[4pt] &=\int \frac{dt}{\sqrt{t^2+2^2}}dx\\[4pt] \int\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2+a^2}}dx&=\log \vert x+\sqrt{x^2+a^2}\vert+c\\[4pt] &=\log \vert t+\sqrt{t^2+2^2}\vert+c\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{\log \vert \tan x+\sqrt{\tan^2x+4}\vert+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 4

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{dx}{x^2-16}\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} \int \frac{dx}{x^2-16}&=\int\frac{dx}{x^2-4^2}\\[4pt] \int \frac{1}{x^2-a^2}dx&=\frac{1}{2a}\log \left\vert\frac{x-a}{x+a}\right\vert+c\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{\frac{1}{8}\log \left\vert\frac{x-4}{x+4}\right\vert+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 5

Integrate$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{1}{x^2+2x+2}dx.\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &=\int \frac{1}{x^2+2x+1-1+2}dx\\[4pt] &=\int \frac{1}{{(x+1)}^2+1^2}dx\\[4pt] \int \frac{1}{{x}^2+a^2}dx&=\frac{1}{a}\tan^{-1}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)+c\\[4pt] x&=x+1\\[4pt] a&=1\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{\frac{1}{1}\tan^{-1}\left(\frac{x+1}{1}\right)+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 6

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{x}{(x+1)(x+2)}dx\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} \frac{x}{(x+1)(x+2)}&=\frac{A}{x+1}+\frac{B}{x+2}\\[4pt] \frac{x}{\cancel{(x+1)(x+2)}}&=\frac{A(x+2)+B(x+1)}{\cancel{(x+1)(x+2)}}\\[4pt] x&=A(x+2)+B(x+1)\\[4pt] \text{Put }x&=-1\\[4pt] -1&=A(1)\\[4pt] A&=\underline{\underline{-1}}\\[4pt] \text{Put }x&=-2\\[4pt] -2&=B(-1)\\[4pt] -1-B&=-2\\[4pt] B&=\underline{\underline{2}}\\[4pt] \int \frac{x}{(x+1)(x+2)}dx&=\int\frac{-1}{x+1}dx+\int\frac{2}{x+2}dx\\[4pt] &=-\int\frac{1}{x+1}dx+2\int\frac{1}{x+2}dx\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{-\log\left\vert x+1\right\vert+2\cdot \log\left\vert x+2\right\vert+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 7

$$\text{Evaluate: }\begin{equation}\color{yellow}{\int_2^3 \frac{x}{1+x^2}dx}\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &\color{yellow}{1+x^2=t}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{0+2xdx=dt}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{2xdx=dt}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{xdx=\frac{dt}{2}}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{t=1+x^2}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{x=2}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{t=1+2^2}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{=\underline{\underline{5}}}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{x=3}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{t=1+3^2}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{t=1+9}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{=\underline{\underline{10}}}\\[4pt] &=\int\frac{dt/2}{t}\\[4pt] &=\frac{1}{2}\int_{\color{yellow}{5}}^{\color{yellow}{10}}\frac{dt}{t}\\[4pt] &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\log{\left\vert t\right\vert}\right)^{10}_5\\[4pt] &=\frac{1}{2}\left(\log10-\log5\right)\\[4pt] \color{yellow}{\log a-\log b}&\,\color{yellow}{=\log\frac{a}{b}}\\[4pt] &=\frac{1}{2}\log\frac{10}{5}\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{\frac{1}{2}\log 2}} \end{align}$$

Question 9

$$\begin{equation}\text{Evaluate }\int \left(2x^2+5x+3\right)dx\end{equation}$$

Question 10

$$\begin{equation}\text{Find }\int \frac{e^{\tan^{-1}x}}{1+x^2}dx\end{equation}$$

$$\begin{align} &\text{Let }\quad\color{yellow}{t=\tan^{-1}x}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\frac{dt}{dx}=\frac{1}{1+x^2}}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{dt=\frac{dx}{1+x^2}}\\[4pt] \int \frac{e^{\tan^{-1}x}}{1+x^2}dx&=\int e^t\cdot dt\\[6pt] &=e^t+c\\[6pt] &=\underline{\underline{e^{\tan^{-1}x}+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 11

$$\begin{align}&(\text{i})\quad\int \frac{dx}{x^2+a^2}=\ldots\\[6pt] &(\text{ii})\quad \text{Evaluate }\int\frac{dx}{x^2-6x+7} \end{align}$$

Question 12

$$\begin{equation}\text{Evaluate}\quad\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-4x^2}}dx \end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &=\int\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-4x^2}}dx\\[4pt] &=\int\frac{1}{\sqrt{4(\frac{1}{4}-x^2)}}dx\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\sqrt{a\cdot b}=\sqrt{a}\cdot\sqrt{b}}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\sqrt{4}=2}\\[4pt] &=\int\frac{1}{\sqrt{4}\cdot\sqrt{\frac{1}{4}-x^2}}dx\\[4pt] &=\int\frac{1}{2\cdot\sqrt{\frac{1}{4}-x^2}}dx\\[4pt] &=\frac{1}{2}\int\frac{dx}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{4}-x^2}}\\[4pt] &=\frac{1}{2}\int\frac{dx}{\sqrt{\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^2-x^2}}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\int\frac{dx}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}=\sin^{-1}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)+c}\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{\frac{1}{2}\sin^{-1}\left( \frac{x}{\frac{1}{2}}\right)+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 42

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{x^2+2}{x^2+1}dx \end{equation}$$

Question 43

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{x^4}{x^2+1}dx \end{equation}$$

Question 45

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{\sin^2x}{(1+\cos x)^2}dx \end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &=\int \frac{1-\cos^2x}{(1+\cos x)^2}dx\\[4pt] &\text{Using the Pythagorean identity:}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\sin^2x=1-\cos^2 x}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{a=1}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{b=\cos x}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{a^2-b^2=(a-b)(a+b)}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{1^2-\cos^2x=(1-\cos x)(1+\cos x)}\\[4pt] &=\int \frac{(1-\cos x)(\cancel{1+\cos x})}{(1+\cos x)^{\cancel{2}}}dx\\[4pt] &\text{Using a half-angle identity:}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{1-\cos x=2\sin^2\frac{x}{2}}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{1+\cos x=2\cos^2\frac{x}{2}}\\[4pt] &=\int \frac{\cancel{2}\sin^2\frac{x}{2}}{\cancel{2}\cos^2\frac{x}{2}}dx\\[4pt] &\text{In terms of sine and cosine:}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\tan^2x=\frac{\sin^2x}{\cos^2x}}\\[4pt] &=\int\tan^2\frac{x}{2}dx\\[4pt] &\text{Using the Pythagorean identity:}\\[4pt] &\color{yellow}{\tan^2x=\sec^2x-1}\\[4pt] &=\int(\sec^2\frac{x}{2}-1)dx\\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{2\tan \frac{x}{2}-x+c}} \end{align}$$

Question 47

$$\begin{equation}\text{Find }\int \frac{x^2+1}{x^2-5x+6}dx \end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} &=\int \frac{x^2-5x+6+5x-6+1}{x^2-5x+6}dx\\[4pt] &=\int \frac{\cancel{x^2-5x+6}}{\cancel{x^2-5x+6}}+\frac{5x-5}{x^2-5x+6}dx\\[4pt] &=\int 1+\frac{5x-5}{x^2-5x+6}dx\\[4pt] &\frac{5x-5}{(x-2)(x-3)}=\frac{A}{x-2}+\frac{B}{x-3}\\[4pt] &\frac{5x-5}{(x-2)(x-3)}=\frac{A}{\cancel{x-2}}(\cancel{x-2})(x-3)+\frac{B}{\cancel{x-3}}(x-2)(\cancel{x-3})\\[4pt] &5x-5=A(x-3)+B(x-2)\\[4pt] &5x-5=Ax-3A+Bx-2B\\[4pt] &5x-5=Ax+Bx-3A-2B\\[4pt] &5x - 5 = (A + B)x - (3A + 2B)\\[4pt] &A + B = 5\tag{1}\\[4pt] &-3A - 2B = -5\tag{2}\\[4pt] &\frac{5x - 5}{(x - 2)(x - 3)} = \frac{-5}{x - 2} + \frac{10}{x - 3}\\[4pt] &\int \frac{x^2 + 1}{x^2 - 5x + 6} \, dx = \int 1 \, dx + \int \frac{-5}{x - 2} \, dx + \int \frac{10}{x - 3} \, dx\\[4pt] &=\boxed{x-5\log\vert x-2\vert+10\log\vert x-3\vert+c} \end{align}$$

From \(A + B = 5\), \(B = 5 - A\). Substitute into the second equation: \[ -3A - 2(5 - A) = -5, \] \[ -3A - 10 + 2A = -5, \] \begin{align} -A &= 5 \\[4pt] A &= \color{yellow}{-5}. \end{align} Then: \begin{align} B &= 5 - (-5) \\[4pt] B&= \color{yellow}{10} \end{align}

Question 49

$$\begin{equation}\int \frac{1-\cos x}{1+\cos x}dx\end{equation}$$

Answer

$$\begin{align} \int \frac{1-\cos x}{1+\cos x}dx &=\int \frac{2\sin^2\frac{x}{2}}{2\cos^2\frac{x}{2}}dx \\[4pt] &=\int\tan^2\frac{x}{2}dx \\[4pt] &=\int\left(\sec^2\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)-1\right)dx \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{1+\cos x=2\cos^2\frac{x}{2}} \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{1-\cos x=2\sin^2\frac{x}{2}} \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{\sec^2x=\tan^2x+1} \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{\tan^2x=\sec^2x-1} \\[4pt] &\text{for }\textcolor{yellow}{\int\sec^2\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)dx} \\[4pt] &\text{Let }\textcolor{yellow}{u=\frac{x}{2}} \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{du=\frac{1}{2}dx} \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{dx=2du} \\[4pt] &\textcolor{yellow}{\int\sec^2\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)dx=2\int \sec^2udu=2\tan u=2\tan\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)} \\[4pt] &=\underline{\underline{2\tan\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)-x+c}} \end{align}$$
